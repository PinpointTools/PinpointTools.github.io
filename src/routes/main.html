Welcome To Pinpoint Tools

Who are we?

We make software and tools/utils for other apps! We're dedicated to make software and tools for other software. Our apps are free and open source! We hate closed-source, even if it's good.

What's so different?

We like simplicity, free, open source, and privacy. Your privacy? Not shared and always local. Your expressive thoughts in our UI? Simple, no bullshit. Broke as hell and want to learn? Code is free and open source forever. That's what open source means.

What do we benefit from this?

Nothing. Other than followers and stars, nothing. We earn 0 dollars from making tools and software. If you wish to donate, you can either do it in Discord Nitro or something else. Some developers might have already have a Paypal/Ko-fi/Patreon link.
